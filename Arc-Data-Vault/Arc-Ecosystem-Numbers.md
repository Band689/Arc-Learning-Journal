# Mapping Arc's Ecosystem by the Numbers 
# 用数据拆解 Arc 的生态版图

## English Version

I spent some time looking through RootData's map of Arc's 104 partners, and it left me with a very different impression than I expected.

Most new chains launch first and then spend years trying to attract developers, liquidity, and eventually businesses. Arc seems to be flipping that order by putting a large ecosystem together before the network is even fully live. Whether that strategy pays off is still an open question, but it's definitely different.

One thing I noticed is how the six layers build on each other instead of feeling like random partnership announcements.

### 1. Asset Issuance
It starts with the assets themselves. Stablecoin issuers like **AllUnity, BDACS, Bitso/Juno, and Stablecorp**, together with tokenization players like **Centrifuge, Securitize, and WisdomTree**, suggest Arc wants real assets on-chain from the beginning rather than years later.

### 2. Infrastructure
This layer isn't the most exciting to talk about, but it's probably one of the most important. Seeing **Blockdaemon, QuickNode, Chainalysis, Elliptic, and DRPC** tells me they're thinking about reliability and compliance early instead of treating them as an afterthought.

### 3. Developer Tools
The builder stack is pretty complete already. **Chainlink, Wormhole, LayerZero, MetaMask, Fireblocks, Privy, Alchemy, and Axelar** cover most of the tools developers would expect. Less time rebuilding infrastructure usually means teams can focus on their products.

### 4. Trading
Liquidity matters just as much as technology. **Coinbase, Kraken, Bybit, Robinhood, Galaxy, and B2C2** give me more confidence that assets won't exist in isolation if adoption grows.

### 5. Payments
> 💡 *This is probably the layer I'm watching most closely.* 

**Visa, Mastercard, Nuvei, Ramp, PhotonPay, and EBANX** make the ecosystem feel much closer to real-world payments than a typical crypto-only network.

### 6. Financial Services
Seeing **BlackRock, Goldman Sachs, HSBC, and State Street** listed alongside **Aave, Morpho, Maple, and BitGo** was probably the biggest surprise for me. It's rare to see traditional finance and DeFi presented as part of the same picture.

### Key Takeaway: USDC as Native Gas ⛽
One thing I also keep coming back to is **USDC being used as the native gas token**. It feels like a small detail, but if Arc wants to reach users outside crypto, removing that extra layer of complexity could actually matter more than people think.

Of course, partnerships alone don't guarantee success. Execution is what matters from here. Still, I think the ecosystem map is worth paying attention to because it shows what Arc is trying to build, not just what it's saying.

## 中文版 (Chinese Version)

最近花时间研究了 RootData 整理的 Arc 104 个生态合作伙伴版图，得到的启发与我之前的预期完全不同。

大多数新公链的路径是：先上线、发币，然后花几年时间艰难地去吸引开发者、流动性和应用场景。而 Arc 似乎完全反过来了——在网络还没有完全公开上线之前，就已经拼好了如此庞大的生态拼图。这种策略最终能否成功还需时间检验，但这绝对是一个不一样的玩法。

我注意到，这六个层级之间并不是零散的、为了发新闻而宣的合作，而是层层递进、逻辑自洽的：

### 1. 资产发行层 (Asset Issuance)
一切始于资产本身。稳定币发行商（如 **AllUnity、BDACS、Bitso/Juno、Stablecorp**）以及现实世界资产（RWA）代币化先锋（如 **Centrifuge、Securitize、WisdomTree**）的加入，表明 Arc 希望从一开始就把真实的金融资产带到链上，而不是等几年之后。

### 2. 基础设施层 (Infrastructure)
这一层或许听起来不够酷炫，但却是最稳固的底座。**Blockdaemon、QuickNode、Chainalysis、Elliptic、DRPC** 的加入，说明团队很早就把安全、合规与节点稳定性放在了首位，而不是出了问题再补救。

### 3. 开发者工具层 (Developer Tools)
工具套件已经非常完备。**Chainlink、Wormhole、LayerZero、MetaMask、Fireblocks、Privy、Alchemy、Axelar** 几乎涵盖了开发者所需的一切。底层工具越好用，应用层团队就能越专注在自己的产品上。

### 4. 交易层 (Trading)
技术再好，没有流动性也是空谈。**Coinbase、Kraken、Bybit、Robinhood、Galaxy、B2C2** 的入驻，确保了生态资产一旦流转起来，就有足够的二级市场承接，不会成为“孤岛”。

### 5. 支付层 (Payments)
> 💡 *这是我个人最关注的一层。*

**Visa、Mastercard、Nuvei、Ramp、PhotonPay、EBANX** 的大规模接入，让这个生态比任何一条纯 Crypto 链都更接近真实世界的日常支付场景。

### 6. 金融服务层 (Financial Services)
将 **BlackRock（贝莱德）、Goldman Sachs（高盛）、HSBC（汇丰）、State Street（道富）** 与 **Aave、Morpho、Maple、BitGo** 放在同一张图谱里，这可能是最让我惊喜的部分。传统金融（TradFi）与去中心化金融（DeFi）真正实现了同频共振。

### 核心观察：USDC 充当原生 Gas ⛽
我一直高度关注的一个细节是 **USDC 将作为原生 Gas 费用**。这看似只是个小改动，但如果 Arc 的终极目标是圈外（Web2）的增量用户，这种“消除额外兑换步骤”的丝滑体验，其意义可能远超很多人的想象。

当然，光有合作伙伴并不代表绝对的成功，未来的关键还在于落地和执行力。但这张生态图谱无疑向我们展示了 Arc 正在默默建造的“经济操作系统（Economic OS）”的真实野心。
